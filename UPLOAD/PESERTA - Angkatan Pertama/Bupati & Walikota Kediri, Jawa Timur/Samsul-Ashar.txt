Born in Kediri on September 16, 1961. He is a medical doctor by training and practice. Upon graduating from medical school at Brawijaya University in 1989, he served as a junior medical doctor in Aceh Province. In 2000, he continued his study to become an internist. Beginning in 2006, he served as Deputy Director of Gambiran Public Hospital in Kediri. Samsul Ashar was democratically elected as Mayor of Kediri in 2008 local elections (pemilukada).
